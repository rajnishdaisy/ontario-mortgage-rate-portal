Manual AI workflow

How the sheet moves

  1. IntakeCapture lender, source, file type, effective date, and uploader context.
  2. AI analysisExtract terms, amortization, fixed/variable rows, Prime, hold period, conditions, and notes.
  3. Lender matchMap clean rows back to the matching lender profile and flag conflicts for admin review.
  4. Publish gateAdmin approves before rows are pushed into the live Rate Board.
