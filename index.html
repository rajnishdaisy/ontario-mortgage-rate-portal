Rate access

Permissioned rate intake, not public scraping.

Broker rates are gated and time-sensitive. The safe operating model is to ingest authorized rate sheets, normalize them, and require human approval before publishing.

A Daily rate-sheet inboxCreate rates@ / desk@, subscribe to lender and BDM bulletins, parse PDF/XLS/HTML attachments, and detect changes.
B Broker portal exportsWhere allowed, save portal exports/rate sheets into a watched folder for automatic extraction and source tracking.
C LOS/network platformsRequest permitted export/API/feed options from Finmo, Velocity, Filogix Expert, M3/BOSS, and lender networks.
D Approval + stale warningsEvery imported rate needs source file, effective date, product type, reviewed by, and expiry/stale warning.
